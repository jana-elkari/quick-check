Welcome to the RE Quick Check

The Quick Check Requirements Engineering (RE) is a lightweight tool that allows practitioners to compare the current state of their RE practices with those of other organizations.

Instructions

  1. This Quick Check is completely anonymous. Your responses cannot be attributed to you or your organization.
  2. Click on "Let's go" to start the Quick Check.
  3. Answer all questions honestly for the most useful feedback.
  4. After submission, you will receive a graphical evaluation of your results.
  5. You can refine comparisons using filters after viewing results.
